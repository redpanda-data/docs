=== knowledge base
:term-name: knowledge base
:hover-text: A structured collection of information that AI agents can query to retrieve relevant context for decision-making.
:category: Agentic Data Plane

Knowledge bases store domain-specific information, documentation, or data that agents can search and retrieve to augment their responses. They enable agents to access up-to-date information beyond their training data and provide context-aware answers based on organizational knowledge.
