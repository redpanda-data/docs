Use Redpanda Console to edit the configuration of existing topics in a cluster.

1. Go to **Topics** in the menu.

1. Select a topic and open the **Configuration** tab.

1. Click the pencil icon in the row of the property that you want to edit.

1. Make your changes and click **Save changes**.
